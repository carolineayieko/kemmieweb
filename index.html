EKEZA KWA MSICHANA

Who are we

Ekeza kwa msichana is a mentorship program that helps young women to realize their potential through the use technology.
The program is focused on helping girls learn skills that are not taught in school and are needed in the outside world.
These skills include; technical skills, entrepreneurial skills, scholarship writing skills, professional development and personal development.
Ekeza is focused on girls in rural and sub-urban areas in coastal region, south western Kenya and rift valley region.
Ekeza Kwa Msichanastrong means is a combination of Swahili words. Ekeza means invest , kwa means in, Msichana means girl,
hence invest in the girl. So we are investing time and resources to empower girls.

Our Mission

Ekeza Kwa Msichana (EKM)envisions a community where young girls are innovative and have essential skills necessary and are able to access various opportunities.
EKM aims to empower young girls, especially the less fortunate, in acquiring education and technical and entrepreneurial skills they can use
to improve their lives and that of their community.

Our Vision

To give back as much as possible to our society by empowering the young girl to believe in themselves
and step out of their comfort zone to acquire education and entrepreneurship skills.

Our Core Values

Commitment, consistency in addition to working hard and smart builds a character of patience and persistence.

Our Core Pillars

entrepreneurial skills: mentees are equipped with entrepreneurial skills and are given a platform to be creative and innovative in coming up with solutions to solve problems in their communities.
Life skills: entails emotional intelligence , use of design thinking when solving socio-economic problems,
time management and conflict management, interpersonal relationship and friendship, coping with pressure and standing strong, creative thinking
scholarship awareness and scholarship essay writing skills:The mentees in high school and university are made aware of various opportunities and are trained to write award-winning scholarship essays and highly encouraged to apply to the numerous funding opportunities both locally and internationally.
professional development: The mentees take part in hands-on workshops that hone their professional communication skills, CV-writing, preparation for tech interviews, writing skills, and academic writing.
personal development: The mentees take part in seminars that improve their personal skills such as confidence building, overcoming Impostor Syndrome, critical thinking and financial literacy.
