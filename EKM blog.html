Emotional intelligence

Posted by carol kemmie on 5 May

Emotional intelligence or EI is the ability to understand and manage your own emotions,
and those of the people around you. People with a high degree of emotional intelligence
know what they're feeling, what their emotions mean, and how these emotions can affect other people.

Read More

Five key elements to it.

  1. Self-awareness
  2. Self-regulation
  3. Motivation
  4. Empathy
  5. Social skills
